About Me

My name is Annie-Stewart Elijah, but I mostly go by my pseudonym, Alviss Raghnall. I mostly use Typescript, Javascript && Java, building scalable (Full Stack) Web, and Blockchain Applications, but have recently begun delving into Rust, as well as GoLang. A citizen of the Giant African Nation, Nigeria, I reside just at the uplands of the Atlantic Ocean.

I'm currently a student at the Rivers State University, and my major is in Electronics Engineering. Love to work out basic Electronics devices and systems (it's all i could do right now), and you could absolutely hit me up if you wanna talk Transistors.

In my free time, I play, watch, and/or talk about football with fellow enthusiasts, and would chant as though I was physically a fragment of 'The Black Wall'.

For the rest of my other 'free' free time, I brush up on my Physics, or listen to good muzik (my sincere apologies). But with GZA, I could do both, and that simply entails the Genius of the Genius. I focus on putting breathe to client ideas; bringing them to reality by taking my time engaging big screens in my work of building these applications, leveraging my skills garnered over years of continuous practise & personal development.
